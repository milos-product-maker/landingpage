Strategic Context

The Privacy Barrier

Healthcare institutions and pharmaceutical companies sit on massive datasets that could revolutionize medicine, but HIPAA, GDPR, and tribal data silos prevent effective collaboration. The cost of data breaches (averaging $9.7M in healthcare) creates a paralyzed environment where the risk of sharing often outweighs the reward of discovery.

Lucid enables "Confidential Collaborative Learning." Multiple entities can train a shared AI model on their private data without the data ever leaving their respective firewalls. We replace legal data transfer agreements with technical hardware guarantees.

The Technical Mechanism

Enclave-Isolated Training:

Model training happens inside a TEE. Even the hospital administrators hosting the hardware cannot view the patient data being processed.

Zero-Knowledge Output:

Only the final, aggregated model weights are released. Individual patient records are cryptographically protected from extraction.

Full Regulatory Audit:

Provide HIPAA auditors with hardware-signed evidence that the data stayed encrypted during the entire compute lifecycle.
