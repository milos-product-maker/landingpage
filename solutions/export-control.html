The Strategic Context

Zero-Overhead Compliance

Advanced AI clusters face strict export controls, but current audit methods are failing. Manual "pen and paper" inspections are too slow for 100,000+ chip sites, while hardware-heavy solutions like GPS are expensive and easy to spoof. This regulatory friction stalls deployments and creates massive operational overhead.

Lucid replaces disruptive audits with real-time, network-rooted compliance. By leveraging existing infrastructure for cryptographic verification, we provide continuous proof of location and end-use for large-scale clusters—with zero impact on performance.

The Technical Mechanism

Network-Rooted Truth:

Continuous verification via existing network infrastructure, providing spoof-proof location and identity without new hardware.

Workload Identity:

Cryptographic assurance that only approved model architectures are being trained or served, satisfying end-use requirements.

Zero-Overhead Monitoring:

Real-time compliance monitoring for clusters of 100,000+ chips with zero impact on compute performance.
