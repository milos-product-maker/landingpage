The Strategic Context

The Jurisdictional Gap

Selling AI across borders is often stalled by conflicting regulations. Even when models are deployed within a target region, customers fear extra-judicial access and non-compliance with local laws. This lack of trust shrinks your addressable market and creates multi-year sales cycles.

Lucid bridges this gap with AI Passports. By establishing a "Digital Embassy"—a technical enclave that operates under verifiable compliance regardless of its physical location—we provide the cryptographic proof required to sell into even the most strictly regulated jurisdictions.

The Technical Mechanism

Cryptographic Handshake:

Data is never decrypted until hardware provides proof of running the authorized, unmodified workload.

RAM Isolation:

Hardware-level encryption ensures that even with physical access, data in memory remains encrypted.

Key Sovereignty:

Encryption keys are managed exclusively by the client and never touch the provider's infrastructure.
