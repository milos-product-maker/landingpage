Behind the Verification

Lucid's architecture rests on three pillars described in our research. Explore the data we collect to generate the Passport.

Pillar 1: Sovereignty

Network Triangulation

We use secure ping-based delays from multiple TEE verifiers to prove the physical location of the workload, countering VPN spoofing.

Source: ICDC 2025 Submission. Proves location within country borders.
Pillar 2: Integrity

Attestable Audits

Safety benchmarks run inside the TEE. We verify the model's responses against standard datasets (MMLU) without leaking weights.

Source: arXiv:2506.23706v1. Verified accuracy & toxicity scores.
Pillar 3: Confidentiality

Pod-as-TEE Architecture

The workload runs in a Confidential Container. Memory is encrypted by the CPU. The "Passport" is only issued if the TEE report is valid.
